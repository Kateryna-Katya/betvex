ABOUT

The Money Minter is more than a reel system — it’s a machine with a reputation. People say it's like if Fort Knox had a baby with some 24/7 factory. Weird comparison but it kinda works. Every symbol feels like a fragment of the machine’s inner workings: piles of banknotes, heavy gold bars, polished coins, and sacks filled to their brim.

Your job? Get every bit of treasure out of this thing.

FEATURES

Money Minter Core Mechanics
It's a 5-reel setup, and each reel is loaded with money-themed symbols - coins, gold, cash, fancy vault stuff.

Symbol Interactions
Certain clusters trigger bursts of animated “money fountains” — cool effects that fill your screen with coins and cash.

Scatter Symbol

Money Minter Respins activate when 3 or more Scatter symbols appear anywhere on the reels. These symbols don’t follow a strict pattern — they simply need to make their presence known on the board.

Under that:
When those Scatters hit, they're stuck there - kinda like heavy coins that just got stamped down.
